Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

What is StewardPro and who is it for?

StewardPro is a comprehensive church management system built specifically for Seventh-day Adventist churches in Tanzania. It's a cloud-based platform that helps churches manage their members, track tithes and offerings, communicate with congregations, and generate reports—all in one secure system. Whether you're a small church with 50 members or a large congregation with thousands, StewardPro scales to meet your needs. It's designed for pastors, church treasurers, clerks, elders, and church administrators who want to modernize their church operations while maintaining SDA-specific practices and reporting requirements.

Can StewardPro generate the reports required by our conference?

Yes! StewardPro is designed with SDA church structure in mind. Our Premium plan includes pre-formatted conference reporting tools that generate the membership, financial, and statistical reports required by East-Central Africa Division conferences.

Is StewardPro difficult to learn? Do we need IT staff?

StewardPro is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive. We provide training and support to help your church get started. You don't need IT staff to use StewardPro.

Can StewardPro integrate with other services we use?

Yes! StewardPro is built with integration in mind, especially for the Tanzanian market

How does StewardPro protect our sensitive church and member information?

StewardPro is built on a secure cloud platform that uses industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. We also offer customizable security settings to meet your specific needs.
